# Changelog

## 0.1.0 — Initial Fork

Forked from [gstack](https://github.com/pawan/pstack) v0.13.3.0.

### What changed

- **New ethos:** Revenue Before Perfection, Ship Then Iterate, Don't Build What You Can Buy, One Person One Repo, Search Before Building.
- **Stripped 9 skills** that don't fit solo builders: codex, cso, autoplan, retro, canary, benchmark, connect-chrome, setup-browser-cookies, design-shotgun, document-release.
- **Kept 19 skills** that matter: office-hours, plan-ceo-review, plan-eng-review, plan-design-review, design-consultation, design-review, review, investigate, qa, qa-only, browse, ship, land-and-deploy, setup-deploy, careful, freeze, guard, unfreeze, pstack-upgrade.
- **Renamed** all gstack references to pstack.
- **New README** focused on bootstrappers, indie hackers, solo founders.
